Gas Flare Down: Infrastructures, Technology Selection And Monetization Strategies

  • Overview
  • Curriculum

Course Description

This course has been carefully designed to address the knowledge gap required in the implementation of a flare down program. The course will provide venture managers and supervisors with the insight required to initiate and execute a gas unitization project as a profitable venture.

Most people wonder why several Billion Cubic Feet (BCF) of natural gas is still being flared on daily basis worldwide despite the vast uses of gas as a cleaner energy source,feedstock for industries and for commercial and residential purposes.

Getting natural gas from the wellhead to the market require huge investment. Of recent, policy makers have introduced different kinds of incentive which is now encouraging the monetisation of both flare gas and the natural gas from small field.

The course will further advice and guide participants on best to choose the best monetization options from various options.
