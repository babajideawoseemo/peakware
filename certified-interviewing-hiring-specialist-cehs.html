Certified Interviewing & Hiring Specialist [CEHS]

  • Overview
  • Curriculum

Course Description

Hiring is one of the very important tasks for HR professionals. Interviewing is not the job of HR professionals only. Any professional who conducts Interview must know the pros and cons of people and interview technique. The cost of wrong hire is very high and hidden cost for the company with certified interviewing & hiring specialist

To ensure the Right Fit for the company, the team responsible for hiring must know and practice the right method for hiring, proper interviewing candidate, right selection. This Certified Interviewing & Hiring Specialist program will ensure these essential requirements of perfect hiring for organizational effectiveness and profitability through people utilization.

Objective:
After successful completion of the program, participants will be able to:

  • Screen the candidate based on required competency
  • Expert in Behavioral Interview
  • Design and develop right question to assess the competency
  • Understand the people psychology
  • Minimize the cost of wrong hire
  • Conduct interview for selecting the right person
  • Hire the RIGHT fit for the company
