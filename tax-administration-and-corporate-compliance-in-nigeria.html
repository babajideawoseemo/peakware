Tax Administration & Corporate Compliance in Nigeria

  • Overview
  • Curriculum

Course Description

Learning the art of Taxation is important for business for compliance with Governmental regulatory and equally for the health of the business against over taxation, double or triple taxation in the quest of compliance. Dealing with tax computation and filing return to the tax body requires some level of knowledge, skill and expertise.

This course has been specially designed to assist candidates to overcome obstacles as well as bridge the gap in their knowledge of up-to-date tax management methods in Nigeria as of today. Deep insight will be shared for tax implication for product and Services across many industries giving you sound understanding of tax compliance obligation. You will learn how to plan and comply with corporate tax law in Nigeria for the Business and for your employees. There will also be knowledge sharing on tax authorities, their powers and operations.
