Certified Human Resources Generalist [CHRG]

  • Overview
  • Curriculum

Course Description

Certified Human Resources Generalist – CHRG is an Intermediate level globally recognized Certification program of Highly Keen that links the theory of HR with the practical HR practices of different sectors. This certification is helpful and suitable for young HR Professionals/ fresher’s to uplift or to get their job as HR Generalist / HR Business Partner / HR Operations. After successful completion of evaluation and examination, you will be getting Internationally Accredited and Globally Recognized Certification from International Professional Accreditation Council – Singapore.

These Certified Human Resources Generalist are developed according to defined international standards and based on the country specific requirement. This certification will cover from very entry level HR function to mid level HR function/ role in different sectors/ industries.

Objective

After successful completion of the program, participants will be able to:

  • Acquire essential knowledge and skills about HR Functions
  • Accomplish core HR function confidently
  • Maintain good interpersonal relationship for organizational excellence
  • Execute proper documentation and compliance issues
  • Manage all the functions for employee life cycle management

Target Person:

  • HR Professionals [ 3 years plus experience in HR]
  • Non HR People [having 8 years professional experience along with attached with HR department in supervisory level]
