  • Overview

Course Description

Nigeria Local content management course is the development of local skills, oil and gas technology transfer, and use of local manpower and local manufacturing in the Nigeria Oil and Gas industry

Local Content Management in the Oil and Gas Industry training seminar will discuss the business benefits of developing the primary components of a local content strategy, including building local workforce and supplier capacity, integrating local content with business plans and successfully monitoring the programmes. The course will help in the development and monitoring Policy, Framework and Compliance for Nigeria Local Content.
