Certified Human Resources Professional [CHRP]

  • Overview
  • Curriculum

Course Description

Human Resource Management is a recognized profession now a day and is also treated as Business Partner. For assuring the success in HR Role, the professionals should maintain a standard level of Competencies. Considering this, WARD in accreditation from EduQual-UK launched this Certified Human Resources Professional (CHRP) Program.

Certified Human Resources Professional CHRP is a senior level HR designation. Certified Human Resources Professional CHRP has been designed based on 4 competencies: HR Functional excellence, HR Business Partnering, HR Development & Leadership, HR Compliance & Law. CHRP assures standard of professional competencies required to play HR’s role in any company. Professionals holding CHRP by EduQual-UK will be considered a competent professional for the respective country. The curriculum and the assessment areas have been designed and developed based on the particular requirement from different country’s perspective.
