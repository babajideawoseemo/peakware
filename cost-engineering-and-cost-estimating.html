Cost Engineering and Cost Estimating Training

  • Overview
  • Curriculum

Course Description

Our Cost Engineering and Cost Estimating training is designed to provide comprehensive coverage of the fundamentals of project cost estimation. The training will also prepare participants for Certified Professional Estimator (CPE) with American Society of Professional Estimators (ASPE)

We will be covering the importance of cost estimating to the entire project process development lifecycle, and discuss how the classification of estimates is based on the maturity of the project and technical deliverables that have been developed in support of the estimate.

This course covers development and evaluation of capital project investment options; investment risk analysis and decision making; the development of project scope and project execution plans that focus on cost estimate and schedule development; cost/schedule control of projects during execution; and closing the process loop through asset and project knowledge management
